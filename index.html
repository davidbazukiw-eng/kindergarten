Welcome! Here you will find everything you need for your modded PS3 — homebrew apps, custom themes, game stores, and more. All links are tested and safe to use on HEN and CFW consoles.

What is HEN / CFW?

HEN (Homebrew Enabler) lets you run homebrew apps and install PKG files on an unmodified PS3. CFW (Custom Firmware) is a modified firmware that gives full control over your console. Most downloads on this site work with both.
